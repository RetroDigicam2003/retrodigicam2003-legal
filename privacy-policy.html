This Privacy Policy explains what information RetroDigicam 2003 ("the App", "we", "us") collects when you use it, why, and what rights you have. The App is a mobile camera app that recreates the look of early-2000s digital cameras and camcorders. It does not require you to create an account.

1. Your photos and videos stay on your device

The App processes photos and videos entirely on your device. We do not upload your photos, videos, or camera roll to our servers or to any third party. When you tap "Save" the result is written to your device's photo library using the operating system's standard photo-library API; when you tap "Share" the operating system's native share sheet opens and you choose the destination yourself. We never see or receive that content.

2. Permissions the App requests

PermissionWhy we ask for it
CameraTo let you take photos and record video inside the App.
MicrophoneTo record audio for your camcorder videos.
Photo Library (add-only)To save your finished photos and videos to your device's photo library. We request add-only access; the App does not browse your existing library except when you explicitly pick a photo or video to import.

You can withdraw any of these permissions at any time in your device's Settings. The App will tell you how to do so if a permission is denied.

3. Information collected automatically

We use a small number of third-party services to keep the App working reliably and to understand how it's used. None of these services receive your photos, videos, or camera roll content.

ServicePurposeWhat it collects
PostHog (analytics) To understand which features are used, so we can improve the App. Anonymous usage events (e.g. "photo captured", "filter changed"), device type, OS version, country-level location derived from IP address. No account or personal identifier.
Sentry (crash & error reporting) To detect and fix bugs and crashes. Crash logs, stack traces, device type, OS version, app version. Occasionally a crash log may incidentally include a file path or in-memory value; we do not intentionally collect personal content through this channel.
RevenueCat (subscription management) To manage and validate optional Pro subscriptions. An anonymous app-installation ID, subscription/purchase status, and transaction identifiers passed to it by Apple/Google during a purchase. RevenueCat does not receive your name, email, or payment details.
Apple App Store / Google Play (in-app purchases) To process optional Pro subscription payments. Handled entirely by Apple or Google under their own privacy policies. We never see your payment details.

4. Legal basis for processing (GDPR)

Where the GDPR applies, we process the data described above under the following legal bases: legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR) for crash reporting and product-improvement analytics, and performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR) for subscription management.

5. International data transfers

PostHog, Sentry, and RevenueCat may process data on servers located outside the European Economic Area (including the United States). Where this happens, the respective provider relies on appropriate safeguards such as the EU Standard Contractual Clauses. See each provider's own privacy policy for details:

  • PostHog Privacy Policy
  • Sentry Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat Privacy Policy

6. Data retention

Analytics events and crash reports are retained by our processors (PostHog, Sentry) for as long as reasonably needed to fix bugs and understand product usage, and are deleted according to their own retention schedules, typically within 12 months. Subscription/purchase records are retained for as long as needed for accounting and legal-compliance purposes.

7. Your rights

If the GDPR applies to you, you have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data, to object to or restrict certain processing, to data portability, and to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority (in Germany, your regional Datenschutzaufsichtsbehörde). Because the App does not use accounts, most requests can only be fulfilled for data tied to an identifier you can provide us with (e.g. a device or purchase identifier). Contact us at retrodigicam2003@protonmail.com for any request.

8. Children's privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your country) and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
